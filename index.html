As Palestinian-Americans from Gaza, our goal is to support our communities in Gaza. Faced with the limitations of existing platforms like LaunchGood, GoFundMe, etc. that do not allow us to provide direct monetary assistance to the people of Gaza, we have taken a proactive step. Recognizing this challenge, we have established our own website featuring dedicated links for donations. This initiative aims to streamline the process and ensure that support reaches those in need more efficiently.

  5. Tents & Blankets: As temperatures drop and many families displaced, tents and blankets become vital sources of shelter and comfort. Your support will ensure that families in Gaza have the warmth and security they need during cold nights.
